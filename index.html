Why Is It Special

Easy to use

With the Briareus Mobile App, it is easier to manipulate the hand fingers as you like

Powerful Design

We developed the project with a model of a hand called Flexy-Hand made by Gyrobot with the same design as a real hand

Customizability

Through the Briareus Support Tool, you can create predefinitions and collections that let you get the most out of your hand.

Awesome Features

Educational Uses

Through our Support Tool, you can translate any text into American Sign Language.

Entertainment

Embark on an extraordinary journey into the captivating world of Project Briareus, where the only limit is your imagination!

Fast and Simple

Our suite of advanced tools provides you with the ability to efficiently manage Briareus.

Predefinitions

You have the capability to set static positions for Briareus, allowing you to control and maintain specific positions with ease.

Collections

The Support Tool allows you to create and customize hand movements, providing you with the flexibility to design and incorporate precise gestures according to your requirements.

Virtual Database

Our data is stored in a real-time database provided by Firebase, ensuring that your information is consistently updated and readily accessible whenever you need it.
